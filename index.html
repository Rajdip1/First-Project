Missions of COSMOS

COSMOS is developing a satellite internet constellation named Bluelink to provide commercial internet service. In January 2020, the Bluelink constellation became the largest satellite constellation ever launched, and as of April 2022 it comprises 2,107 satellites in orbit. The company is also developing Starship, a privately funded, fully reusable, super heavy-lift launch system for interplanetary and orbital spaceflight. Starship is intended to become Cosmos primary orbital vehicle once operational, supplanting the existing Falcon 9, Falcon Heavy, and Dragon fleet. Starship will have the highest payload capacity of any orbital rocket ever built on its debut, scheduled for 2022
